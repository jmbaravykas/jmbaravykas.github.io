03. Education

Vilnius Gediminas Technical University

Bachelor's Degree of Engineering Informatics

2012 Sep - 2016 Jul

Study programme aims:

1. The graduate has to be ready to design, create and test information systems to the domain requirements, to make a plan and a budged for creating and implementing information systems while using resources optimally.
2. The graduate has to be able to create flexible IT services improving IT productivity based on the results and methodologies of IT management modelling.
3. The graduate must be able to manage requirement conformity and risk in IT environment while guaranteeing data and software integrity.

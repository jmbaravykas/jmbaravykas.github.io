MIRA | Jan 2021 - Dec 2022
Team Lead of Front-End team, Front-End Developer
Mira is an insurance underwriting tool for MidCorp business. It allows underwriters to have an holistic view of one risk based on external data as well as internal portfolio data. Mira uses a set of artificial intelligence algorithms to minimise the manual actions needed by the underwriter.
Technologies: Angular, TypeScript, RxJs, NgRx, Node.js, Cyperss.io, Plotly.js, Bing Maps, SCSS, HTML5.
